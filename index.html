Saturn

Lightning Fast Voice Assistant with Powerful AI Tools

Saturn connects your action button to Google's Gemini AI. A live session starts instantly when you open the app, delivering responses in just 300ms. With 18 specialized iOS tools and access to any shortcut you create, it intelligently combines context and advanced AI to seamlessly chain actions together.
